RECIPE 1: Classic Pancakes
---------------------------
Ingredients:
  - 1 cup all-purpose flour
  - 2 tbsp sugar
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - 1/2 tsp baking soda
  - 1/4 tsp salt
  - 1 cup buttermilk
  - 1 egg
  - 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:
  1. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk wet ingredients separately.
  3. Combine wet and dry — do not overmix.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan on medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  7. Serve warm with maple syrup.

RECIPE 2: Tomato Basil Pasta
------------------------------
Ingredients:
  - 250g spaghetti
  - 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  - 3 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  - 3 tbsp olive oil
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:
  1. Boil pasta until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Sauté garlic for 1 min.
  3. Add tomatoes. Cook for 8–10 minutes.
  4. Toss in pasta. Add pasta water if needed.
  5. Top with fresh basil and cheese.

RECIPE 3: Banana Smoothie
---------------------------
Ingredients:
  - 2 ripe bananas
  - 1 cup milk (or almond milk)
  - 1/2 cup yogurt
  - 1 tbsp honey
  - Pinch of cinnamon

Instructions:
  1. Peel and slice bananas.
  2. Add all ingredients to blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into glasses and serve chilled.
