====================================
       OUR SOLAR SYSTEM — FACTS
====================================

THE SUN
-------
- Type: G-type main-sequence star
- Age: ~4.6 billion years
- Diameter: 1,392,700 km
- Surface Temperature: ~5,500°C
- Core Temperature: ~15,000,000°C

THE PLANETS
-----------
1. Mercury
   - Distance from Sun: 57.9 million km
   - Moons: 0
   - Day length: 59 Earth days
   - Year length: 88 Earth days

2. Venus
   - Distance from Sun: 108.2 million km
   - Moons: 0
   - Hottest planet (~465°C average)
   - Rotates in reverse direction

3. Earth
   - Distance from Sun: 149.6 million km
   - Moons: 1
   - Only known planet with life
   - 71% surface covered by water

4. Mars
   - Distance from Sun: 227.9 million km
   - Moons: 2 (Phobos, Deimos)
   - Home to Olympus Mons (tallest volcano in solar system)

5. Jupiter
   - Distance from Sun: 778.5 million km
   - Moons: 95 known
   - Largest planet — 1,300 Earths could fit inside

6. Saturn
   - Distance from Sun: 1.43 billion km
   - Moons: 146 known
   - Famous for its stunning ring system

7. Uranus
   - Distance from Sun: 2.87 billion km
   - Rotates on its side (98° axial tilt)
   - Has 13 known rings

8. Neptune
   - Distance from Sun: 4.5 billion km
   - Fastest winds in the solar system (~2,100 km/h)
   - 1 year = 165 Earth years
